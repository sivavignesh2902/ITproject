Eligibility Criteria

Inorder to grab admission in our college, the student must satisfy the following Eligibility Criteria

  1. Candidates who have passed Higher Secondary Examination of any recognised board
  2. Candidates who have passed Senior School Certificate Examination (XII standard) of the Central Board of Secondary Examination with not less than five subjects of which English shall be one.
  3. Candidates who have passed ICS Examination of the Council for Indian School Certificate Examination with not less than five subjects, of which English shall be one.
  4. Candidates who have passed any Examination conducted by a State Government or a University or Board under the 10+2 pattern with not less than five subjects, of which English shall be one.
  5. Candidates from foreign contries must have a good score (changes year by year) in SAT( for UG ) and GRE (for PG).Since the medium of instruction will be in english, the students are required to pass with good grade in TOEFL examination
  6. Additional Requirements for Specific Programmes
